Carnitas

Description

Famous Mexican pulled-pork.

Ingredients

  • Small corn tortillas
  • Pork shoulder
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Water
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Season the pork and place in slow cooker with onion and garlic and a little bit of water until fully cooked.
  2. Cut the pork into smaller "shredded" pieces.
  3. Oil a pan with vegetable oil. Fry the tortillas for 1-2 minutes on each side. Repeat for however many carnitas you want.
  4. Put the tortillas on a plate and place the carnitas over the tortillas.
  5. Add desired toppings.
