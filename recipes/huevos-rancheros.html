Huevos Rancheros

Description

Mexican ranchero eggs served for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Small corn tortilla
  • Eggs
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Lightly coat a pan and fry each side of the tortillas for about 1-2 minutes each.
  2. Lightly coat another pan in vegetable oil, and fry however many eggs you want until they are lightly or medium done.
  3. Place the eggs on top of the tortillas on a plate.
  4. Add desired toppings like sourcream or salsa roja.
