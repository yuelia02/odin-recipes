Enchiladas

Description

Enchiladas are corn tortillas filled with chicken covered in salsa de chile serrano. They are easy to make and a great introduction to Mexican cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Chicken
  • Chile serrano
  • Jitomate
  • Garlic
  • Water
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Boil, fry, or bake the chicken until cooked, and then let it cool down.
  2. Take the chicken out and shred it.
  3. Boil the jitomates along with the chile serrano, then pour both of them in a blender with onion and garlic. Once it's finished, cook the sauce in a pan with a little bit of vegetable oil.
  4. Pour vegetable oil over a pan and spread it evenly. Put a rolled tortilla over it and let it fry for 2-3 minutes on each side. Repeat for however many enchiladas you want.
  5. Place the tortillas on a plate, and add the chicken on top each tortilla. Pour the salsa over the enchiladas.
  6. Add desired toppings.
